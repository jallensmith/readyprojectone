Digimon (デジモン Dejimon?), short for "Digital Monster" (デジタルモンスター Dejitaru Monsutā?), are mysterious lifeforms that were discovered in the Computer Network in 1997. Digimon include almost all the creatures that reside within a parallel universe called the "Digital World." They age via a process called "Digivolution" which changes their appearance and increases their physical powers. (More Information Here)
